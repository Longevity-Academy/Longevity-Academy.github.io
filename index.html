Learning Outcomes

By session 12, you will:

Read your metabolic signals intelligently

Understand glucose as a signal — not a villain. See how metabolic dysfunction develops silently and what your body is actually telling you.

Interpret CGM data with clinical literacy

Go beyond the numbers. Distinguish normal variability from real signals, and learn what to notice versus what to ignore in your glucose data.

Design meals based on YOUR glucose responses

Stop thinking in rules. Learn how food order, composition, and context shape your personal response — then build a default meal framework that works for you.

Optimize sleep, stress, and recovery deliberately

See sleep as metabolic regulation. Recognize emotional glucose spikes. Design circadian rhythm habits and personal stress-regulation strategies.

Evaluate supplements and health tech critically

Learn the evidence hierarchy. Distinguish supplements that matter from noise. Build a personal decision framework for health technology and testing.

Build a personalized 90-day longevity plan

Integrate everything into a Personal Longevity Blueprint with concrete actions for nutrition, movement, sleep, stress, and social health — plus a failure plan.

The Curriculum

12 sessions. 4 phases. One complete transformation.

Each session builds on the last. By the end, you won't just know more — you'll think differently about how you age.

Phase 1
Reframe
Sessions 1–3
Session 1 — Reframing Aging
Understand aging as a modifiable trajectory. Articulate personal healthspan goals. Shift from fixing problems to designing systems.
Session 2 — Metabolic Health
Understand glucose as a signal. See how metabolic dysfunction develops silently. Learn why weight alone is a poor health marker.
Session 3 — Nutrition for Longevity
Stop thinking in rules, start thinking in responses. Understand protein, fiber, and processing. Design a default meal framework.
Phase 2
Measure
Sessions 4–6
Session 4 — CGM: Seeing the Invisible
How CGM works, what data means and doesn't. Reduce anxiety around numbers. Learn to observe with curiosity, not judgment.
Session 5 — Food Order & Glucose Curves
How sequence matters. Understand carb context. Reduce spikes without restriction through safe personal experiments.
Session 6 — Movement as a Glucose Tool
Muscle as glucose sink. Post-meal walking and resistance training. Build a realistic, age-appropriate movement plan.
Phase 3
Interpret
Sessions 7–9
Session 7 — Sleep & Circadian Rhythm
Sleep as metabolic regulation. Late eating effects and circadian misalignment. Cortisol, insulin resistance, and sleep debt.
Session 8 — Stress & Emotional Spikes
Recognize non-food glucose spikes. The cortisol-glucose loop. Build a personal stress regulation plan.
Session 9 — Supplements & Health Tech
Avoid biohacking traps. Evidence hierarchy and decision filters. Supplements that matter vs noise.
Phase 4
Build
Sessions 10–12
Session 10 — Social Health & Purpose
Psychosocial predictors of aging. Isolation vs mortality. Purpose as a stress buffer. Design social health habits.
Session 11 — Personal Longevity Blueprint
Integrate everything without overload. Blueprint construction with peer coaching and failure planning.
Session 12 — Reflection & Next 90 Days
Before/after reflections. CGM insights synthesis. Commitment statements and 90-day action plan.
Session Format

Every session follows the same rigorous structure.

This isn't a passive webinar. Every 90 minutes is designed for active learning, real data, and peer discussion.

0–15 min

Review & Discussion

Share insights from between-session work. Surface questions, breakthroughs, and challenges from the previous week.

15–40 min

Expert-Led Teaching

Visual walkthroughs, real data, case studies, and myth-dismantling. The core science and frameworks for this session's topic.

40–65 min

Interactive Workshop

Breakout groups, meal deconstruction, CGM data review. Apply what you just learned to your own life and data.

65–85 min

Group Practice

Peer coaching, personal experiments, troubleshooting. Refine your approach with real-time feedback from the group.

85–90 min

Assignment Preview

Between-session work: lifestyle logs, food experiments, CGM observation, sleep audits, and blueprint-building.

Is This For You?

Designed for a specific kind of learner.

This course is for you if…
  • You want to understand what's actually happening inside your body — not just follow generic health advice
  • You're an adult (35–65) ready to invest time in building evidence-based longevity habits
  • You value live discussion and peer learning over passive video content
  • You're curious about CGM, metabolic health, and longevity science — but want expert guidance interpreting the data
  • You want a structured, cohort-based experience with accountability — not another self-paced course you'll never finish
This course is NOT for you if…
  • You're looking for a medical diagnosis or treatment plan — this is education, not clinical care
  • You want a quick fix or a 30-day transformation promise — real change takes structured effort
  • You prefer passive, pre-recorded video content you can watch on your own schedule
  • You're not willing to participate in group discussion, breakout sessions, and between-session work
